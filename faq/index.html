General Questions

What services does OMO G Trading & Interior Decoration offer?

We specialize in complete interior design solutions including residential and commercial design, custom furniture, gypsum ceilings, 3D wall panels, and turnkey project management. We handle everything from concept to final installation, making your dream space a reality.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We provide an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, budget, and design vision. Contact us via WhatsApp, email (info@omoginteriors.com or bookings@omoginteriors.com) to schedule your complimentary consultation.

Which areas in Qatar do you serve?

We serve all areas of Qatar including Doha, West Bay, The Pearl, Lusail, Al Wakrah, and surrounding regions. No project is too far for us!

How long does a typical interior design project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A single room makeover typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complete villa interiors usually take 2-4 months. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation based on your specific project requirements.

What makes OMO G different from other interior design companies?

We offer true turnkey solutions - managing every aspect from design to installation. With 45+ completed projects, we understand Qatar's luxury market and climate requirements. We also operate in both Qatar (CR No. 235664) and Nigeria, bringing diverse design perspectives and international expertise to every project.

Pricing & Process

How much does interior design cost?

Project costs depend on space size, design complexity, materials, and custom requirements. We provide transparent, detailed quotes after the initial consultation and site measurement. Every project is unique, and we work within various budgets to deliver exceptional results. Contact us for a personalized estimate.

What's included in your turnkey solutions?

Our turnkey service includes: design consultation, 3D renderings, material sourcing, custom furniture fabrication, gypsum ceiling installation, 3D wall panels, complete installation, project management, and final handover. You get one point of contact for everything - no need to coordinate with multiple contractors.

Do you work with contractors and developers?

Absolutely! We collaborate with contractors, developers, and property owners on both residential and commercial projects. We can integrate seamlessly into your existing project team and coordinate with other trades as needed.

What payment terms do you offer?

We typically work on a milestone-based payment schedule: deposit at contract signing, progress payments during key phases (design approval, materials procurement, installation milestones), and final payment upon completion. Specific terms are clearly outlined in your project proposal.

Design & Materials

Can you match specific design styles?

Absolutely! We specialize in modern luxury, contemporary, classic, and Arabic-inspired designs. Share your inspiration images or Pinterest boards, and we'll create a custom design that perfectly reflects your vision while ensuring functionality and comfort.

Do you provide 3D visualizations before starting work?

Yes! We provide detailed 3D renderings so you can visualize the final result before we begin any installation. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the design, materials, colors, and layout. We can make revisions to the design until it's exactly what you want.

What types of materials do you use?

We source high-quality materials specifically suitable for Qatar's climate, including premium gypsum for ceilings, durable 3D wall panels, custom furniture materials, and luxury finishes. All materials are selected for both longevity and aesthetics, ensuring your investment lasts for years.

Can you source specific brands or create custom pieces?

Yes! We can source specific brands and products you prefer, or create completely custom furniture and fixtures tailored to your exact specifications and space requirements. Our custom fabrication capabilities allow us to bring any design vision to life.

Project Management

Will I have a dedicated project manager?

Yes! Every project is assigned a dedicated manager who coordinates all aspects and serves as your main point of contact throughout the entire project. They'll keep you updated on progress, handle any questions, and ensure everything runs smoothly.

What if I want to make changes during the project?

We accommodate reasonable changes, though they may affect timeline and budget depending on the stage of the project. We'll discuss any modifications with you, provide updated quotes and timelines, and get your approval before proceeding with changes.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

For commercial projects, we can assist with necessary permits and coordinate with local authorities in Qatar. For residential projects, we advise on any required approvals and can guide you through the process as needed.

What warranty do you provide?

We stand behind our work with warranties on installation and workmanship. Specific warranty terms (typically 1-2 years depending on the type of work) are provided in your project contract. We also offer ongoing maintenance services to keep your spaces looking beautiful.
