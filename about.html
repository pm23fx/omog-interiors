About OMO G

Excellence in Interior Design Since 2019

Our Story

Building dreams, one space at a time

OMO G Trading and Interior Decoration was founded with a vision to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. Established in Lagos, Nigeria, and now headquartered in the heart of Doha, Qatar, the company has grown into a trusted name in interior and exterior design, recognized for exceptional craftsmanship, innovative solutions, and client focused delivery.

With over five years of industry experience and more than 88 successfully completed projects, OMO G prides itself on meticulous attention to detail, creative excellence, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Our multidisciplinary team blends strong local market knowledge with international design trends to craft spaces that are both elegant and highly functional.

In addition to our Qatar operations, our Lagos branch remains fully active, enabling OMO G to serve clients across West Africa and the Middle East with the same high standards of professionalism, reliability, and design integrity that define our brand.

Meet Our CEO

Visionary leadership driving excellence in design

Mrs Omoefe Eguriase

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Mrs Omoefe Eguriase is an experienced and enthusiastic interior design professional with a passion for transforming spaces into works of art. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of both contemporary and traditional design principles, she has built OMO G Trading And Interior Decoration into one of Qatar and Nigeria most trusted interior decoration companies.

Her innovative approach combines functionality with aesthetic beauty, ensuring every project reflects the unique personality and needs of her clients. Under her leadership, the company has successfully completed numerous residential and commercial projects across Qatar and Nigeria, earning a reputation for excellence and customer satisfaction.
